Waterfalls Scenic Excursion

Located on the southeast coast of southern Belize are the beautiful Stan Creek Hills the home to beautiful havens like this. Bask under the beautiful southern water falls and enjoy some good family fun. Rum punch is an option!

  • Comfortable, air-conditioned transport
  • Water
  • Shoes you are comfortable getting wet
  • Change of clothes
  • Towel
  • Sunscreen
  • Bug spray
