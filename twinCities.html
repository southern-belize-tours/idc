Twin Cities Xunantunich & Cahal Pech

Located in the Western most reaches of Belize are the two mystical and ancient Maya sites of Xunantunich and Cahal Pech. These two structures date back to the Classic period of the Maya and represents the grandeur of the Maya civilization whilst they were at their highest height. Learn about the history of the Maya and the archaeological, astronomical and architectural wonders performed by these Palaeolithic people. Guests will also get to enjoy the beautiful surrounding of the lush rain forests of Belize.

  • An authentic Belizean meal
  • Comfortable, air-conditioned transport
  • Water
  • Sunscreen
  • Hiking or tennis shoes
  • Bug Spray
