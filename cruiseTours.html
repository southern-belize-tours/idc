Set Anchor and Discover

Looking to start an adventure in Placencia for a day while your cruise ship sets anchor? We will have our experienced tour guides pick you up from the Placencia docks, the direct destination of the Harvest Caye express, to start your comfortable, safe, and timely excursion.

See Belize's Untouched Wonders

The most popular way to experience the full beauty of Belize's untouched broadleaf rainforest is through our waterfalls tour. You will see some of the most breath-taking natural wonders on the planet and will be treated to an authentic, fresh Belizian barbeque.

Taste the Spices of Belize

Looking to see and taste the tropical botanics? The spice farm is a great way to spend your time on the main land. You can try the freshest of tropical spices while strolling through groves of Birds of Paradise. The compound features beautiful architecture, hallmarked by the ceiling, featuring the varieties of native Belizian Woods.

Discover the Ancient History of Belize

Or perhaps you are looking to see into ancient Mayan history? We will whisk you away to the fortress and fabled Crystal Skull of Lubaantun, or show you the history of the perfectly preserved ball court of Nim Li Punit. If you are looking for a full day of adventure, these tours can be bundled for a discount!
