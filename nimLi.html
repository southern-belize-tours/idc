Nim Li Punit Mayan Ruins

Nestled between the lush broadleaf rainforest and the the foothills of the Maya mountains, resting between clear mountain streams lies Nim Li Punit. This site is very well-preserved as the relatively high population flourished at its peak around 500 AD.

Nim Li Punit is well-known for the large amount of stelae (stone tablets) commemorating the ancient royalty, culture, and general records of the site. You will also see an astonishingly well-preserved Mayan basketball court near the plaza of this ancient city.
