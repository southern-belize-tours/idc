Inland Blue Hole and Cave

The Mayan shamans used these series of amazing cave systems for their religious ceremonies as they believed this was the ancient dwelling place of their dark lords. The underworld known as Xibalba translated into hell must be traversed by the soul in order to ascend into heaven.

In the modern age we explore these caves and learn and appreciate all about the mayan culture. We top it off with a swim in the natural pool created by a sinkhole in the side of the mountain known as the inland blue hole.

  • An authentic Belizean meal
  • Comfortable, air-conditioned transport
  • Water
  • Shoes you are comfortable getting wet
  • Change of clothes
  • Towel
  • Sunscreen
  • Bug spray
