South Staan Creek River Tubing

River rafting on inner tubes through the foothills of beautiful southern Belize on a crystal clear riverine system through dense, tropical, broadleaf rainforest followed by an authentic delicious Belizean coconut rice & beans meal.

  • An authentic Belizean meal
  • Comfortable, air-conditioned transport
  • Water
  • Shoes you are comfortable getting wet
  • Change of clothes
  • Towel
  • Sunscreen
  • Bug spray
Pricing

$70 USD per person, $140 Belizean Dollars per person. Minimum 2 people per group.

Due to the distance from the Placencia Docks to South Staan Creek, this tour cannot accomodate cruise guests.

Please do not hesitate to contact us about rates for large groups. Book your spots for this tour now to pay a 10% booking deposit, and pay the rest when the tour begins.
