Tours

If you are looking to start an adventure while staying in the Placencia area, you will have a wide selection of outstanding and unique experiences where you can select your niche. Our professional guides will pick you up right from your hotel door, where we will accompany you through the sites, and wherever Belize takes you. Many of these sites that are nearby to another can be bundled together for a discount. If you decide that you want to add a stop throughout your journey, we will be happy to accomodate and to give you the best experience Belize offers. If you have an experience in mind that is not listed in this section, please feel free to contact us and we will look into it for you.

Discover the Ancient History of Belize

Interested in uncovering the mysteries of the ancient Mayans? Visit the stone fortress of Lubantuun, where a crystal skull was found. Or perhaps you can stroll through the deadly basketball arena and translate the Mayan heiroglyphs in Nim Li Punit. In the same day you can marvel at the view from the toweringcentral structure in the lost civic center of Xunantunich, while learning how the Mayan nobles lived it up in their hilltop home of Cahal Pech. If you want passage through the Mayan underworld of Xibalba, our guides will be your ferrymen. There, you will hike through some of the most beautiful jungle paths and tube through a relaxing, perfectly blue river that stretches underground into a subterranean cave system with stalagmites and stalactites that the Mayans believed to be the guardians of the underworld.

See Belize's Untouched Wonders

If the Mayan underworld is insufficient enough to get your adrenaline pumping, you can book a ziplining tour throughout the jungle canopy, and experience breath-taking views of the natural beauty of the rainforest creek. Or, if you want to relax with nature, we can show you some beautiful aquatic wonders in the jungle. Our guides can whisk you away to the jungle waterfalls, where you will be treated to an authentic Belizian meal, or you can cool off in Belize's inland blue hole, and explore the subterranean cave system.

Dip Your Feet into the Caribbean Sand

Perhaps the Caribbean is what you had in mind when you were thinking "cool off." We can take you on a boat ride through the turqoise blue water to South Water Caye, where you can explore an expansive coral reef with its very own South Water Marine Reserve, a must-see snorkeling excursion where you can dry off lounging in Pelican Beach, ranked by Discovery as one of the top 21 best beaches in the world. For a true premier tour, we can book you a private airplane to fly over the Great Blue Hole, where you can marvel at the Caribbean's most beautiful water. If scuba diving thorugh the depths of the Great Blue Hole is on your bucket list, you can contact us and we will arrange your adventure through the depths of the world's largest oceanic blue hole.
